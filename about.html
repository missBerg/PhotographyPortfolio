ABOUT

My name is Dorota Ebert, and I love creating custom photo sessions for maternity, newborn, children, and families - capturing the magic before the moment passes…

My biggest joy is to present you those precious moments as stunning photographs that you and your family will cherish forever.

For maternity and newborn, it’s like the first chapter of an amazing journey you are on. With children and families, it’s all about creating a wonderful snapshot in time which is encapsulated in a frame or album. It's for you to share and enjoy with friends and family.

It all began over 10 years ago, when I started capturing real, everyday moments of joy of friends I love, and the children of families I worked for.

Since my late teens I have been a nanny working with amazing families in the USA and UK, and my passion for photography grew ever since I started working with children.
